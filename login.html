Welcome to: Who likes my posts?

To use this app you need to login to Facebook and authorize it.

Don't worry, we won't collect any of your information. Who likes my posts visualize your Facebook fans (your friends who like your posts the most) and let you explore what have they liked.

To use it please log in using the button above. If you can't see the login button it might be because you have a browser plugin blocking social apps. Please disable your blocking plugins and try again.
